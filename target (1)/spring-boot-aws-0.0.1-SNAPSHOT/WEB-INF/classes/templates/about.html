Dorothy has a wealth of experience working in the hospitality / Food Tourism industry. She is well travelled, from home in Inchigeela, to San Francisco, Brussels and the Beara Pennisula! Dorothy has worked in many award winning hotels and restaurants including her own in the past.

Dorothy has gained extensive training and knowledge of Tour Guiding, Irish Food History and Culture with lots of cooking techniques and tips exclusive to the River Lee Valley.
