What to wear?

Comfortable layer clothing, walking boots / shoes are advisable.

Local Shops?

Local shops are open between 9am-6pm. Supermarkets are open between 8.30am-9pm. ATM's are available in Macroom and the Inchigeela Post Office.

Restroom facilities?

Several bathroom facilities are available on route. They are also available in the Castle Hotel reception and in the town square before set off.

Place to put rubbush?

Bins will be available on the bus. Please recycle any rubbishyou bring and do not litter! Please do not leave personal belongings on the bus.

Merchandise?

Cotton Lee Valley Taste Trail bags are available to buy onboard the bus.

Food provided?

Lunch will not be provided on the tour but it can be purchased at the Castle hotel or for the Friday tour a resevation can be made at the Mills Inn. Complemetary samples will be given during the tour.
